STR AID ELIGIBILITY
- Malaysian citizen
- Monthly household income below RM5000
- Register at Bantuan Tunai Rakyat portal
- Payments disbursed quarterly

DRIVING LICENSE RENEWAL
- Valid IC required
- Pay renewal fee via JPJ portal online
- Renewal allowed 6 months before expiry

ROAD TAX RENEWAL
- Valid insurance required before road tax
- Available online via MyJPJ app

PASSPORT RENEWAL
- Valid IC required
- Upload passport photo online
- Collect at Immigration counter or postal delivery

PTPTN LOAN REPAYMENT
- Log in via i-Akaun
- Minimum repayment RM50month
- Exemptions available for low income earners
